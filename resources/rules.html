Hnefatafl

A simple interpretation of the Fetlar ruleset

back
  1. Objectives:
    • White: escape with the King via moving to one of the corner fields.
    • Black: capture the King by surrounding him on all four sides with black pawns.
  2. Movement:
    • Black moves first, then players take turns - passing is not allowed.
    • All pieces can move on straight lines and are blocked by other pieces.
    • The corner and center tiles are restricted and can only be entered by the King
  3. Capturing:
    • A piece is captured when it ends up neighboring two hostile pieces horizontally or vertically.
    • Capturing only takes part when the trap is closed as result of the aggressors move - a pawn can by itself move into the trap without being taken.
    • The corner and center tiles always count as hostile for the purpose of closing a trap.
  4. Controls:
    • Mouse Left: Select pawn
    • Mouse Right: Commit movement
    • Keyboard R: Reset game server
